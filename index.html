identifying usability problems

cause and effect diagram

findings on accessibility

issues detected with WebAIM WAVE

  • 1. The images in the carousel do not have alternative text for those who cannot see an image or when the image is unavailable.

  • 2. Not only is the small text and thin font difficult for users with low vision abilities to read, the clustered text and lack of simplicity opens up problems for website visitors with dyslexia.

  • 3. With dull color combinations like gray backgrounds and white texts, users will be unable to read the text and identify important information or dates.

  • 4. The document language is not identified, which would help readers to read the content in the appropriate language and facilitate automatic translation of content.
